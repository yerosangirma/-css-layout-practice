Haramaya University

information Haramaya University (HU) is one of Ethiopia's oldest and most prominent public research universities, located in the town of Haramaya in the Oromia Region, approximately 510 km east of Addis Ababa. Established in 1954 as the Imperial Ethiopian College of Agricultural and Mechanical Arts (IECAMA), the university was founded through a partnership between the Ethiopian government and Oklahoma State University under the U.S. Point Four Program. Initially focused on agricultural education, HU has since evolved into a multidisciplinary institution offering a wide range of academic programs. Academic Structure Haramaya University comprises several colleges and institutes, including: College of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences College of Business and Economics College of Computing and Informatics College of Education and Behavioral Sciences College of Health and Medical Sciences College of Law College of Natural and Computational Sciences College of Social Sciences and Humanities College of Veterinary Medicine Haramaya Institute of Technology Academy of Sport Sciences
